R J Jewellers is an established name in the Jewellery business having experience since last 5 generations.

We are the first 100% Hallmarked Jewellery Showroom in Greater Noida. Highly skilled craftsmen create our products along with stringent quality control measures which creates that perfection in the products we sell.

To give every customer much more than what he asks for in terms of quality, selection, value for money and customer service, by understanding local tastes and preferences and innovating constantly keeping in mind the overall designs from all over India to eventually provide an unmatched experience in jewellery shopping. Our exquisite collective of Rings, Chains, Earrings, Bangles , Necklaces, Nath, Maang Tikka, Pendent, Pendent Sets, Bracelets, Baju Band, Tagri etc…. will cover all Jewellery designs from all over India making an unmatched selection of your choice.

R J Jewellers is a Govt. registered Gold Valuer. Our valuation papers are considered for valuation in Income tax.

We deal in all kinds of Gold / Diamond Jewellery, Solitaires, Gemstones, Silver Gift Articles. Few of the products we deal in are as follows –

  • Plain, Antique, Jadau Gold Jewellery
  • IGI and GAI certified Solitares
  • IGI certified Diamond Jewellery
  • Platinum Jewellery
  • Italian Jewellery
  • South Coimbatore Jewellery
  • Kundan Jewellery
